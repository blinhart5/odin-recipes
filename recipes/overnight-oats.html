Overnight Oats

Description

Quick and easy, no cook overnight oats. Easily Customizable

Ingredients

  • Sealable Jar (mason) - 8 oz for this recipe
  • Milk of choice (dairy, almond, oat, coconut, etc,)
  • Oats (rolled or steel cut; dealer's choice)
  • 2 tsp Chia Seeds
  • 1 Tbsp Slivered Almonds
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 Tbsp Shredded Coconut
  • 1 - 2 tsp Sugar or honey (for sweetener)

Steps

  1. Fill Mason Jar 3/4 full with oats
  2. Add dry Ingredients (adjust any amounts to personal tastes/preferences)
  3. Add milk to be level with oats/dry ingredients
  4. Cover with lid and shake until combined
  5. Refigerate 6 hours - overnight. Enjoy!
